High-Spec Robot Roboco-san

Roboco (ロボ子, Robo-ko, lit. Robogirl) also known as "Roboco-san" is a female Japanese virtual YouTuber and the second member ever of hololive.
While she debuted alone way before generations were established, she is currently part of "hololive 0th gen" alongside fellow solo debutants: Tokino Sora, Sakura Miko, Hoshimachi Suisei and AZKi.
